Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one my favorite foods. Here is a receipe for deliciously rich eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a favorite pan over a medium heat
  2. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  3. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  4. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
