From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French tem for an omamental vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the vegetables to enhace the garden's beauty.

What to plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and dorm. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formaly of a knot garden.
